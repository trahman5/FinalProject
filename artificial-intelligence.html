Welcome to Using Generative Artificial Intelligence webpage

On this webpage, I will cover about Generative Artificial Intelligence, it has been a huge controversial topic throughout the internet for many years as different models began to release to the public for them to use, however the way it has been used has been an issue as many people claim and worry about generative artificial intelligence models being abused, spreading misinformation while also promoting unethical issues in additions to how it affects society such as people using the models for plagiarism for work and other academical uses. Especially using them to do coding for them, specifically for web design, as that will both heavily impact the job opportunities and economy for web designers. My take and belief on generative artificial intelligence while it is unethical and wrong in many ways, I believe it can be used more effectively in a positive approach, such as being an assistant rather than doing the entire work for you as it could help point out flaws when it comes to doing code while also suggesting and brainstorming advices, it will allow people to still have the ability to brainstorm and expand their creativity without being too dependent on generative artificial intelligent models and that there could be some restrictions or exceptions how it should be used in terms of academics. I just hope that generative artificial intelligence will be used more properly and correctly, while also having some restrictions, nevertheless.
