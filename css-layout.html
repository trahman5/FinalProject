CSS Layout and Responsive Design webpage

On this webpage, I will cover the fundamentals of Layout and Responsive Design. And how it actually benefits people who are impaired, whether it is visual, cognitive, motor, or auditory, while also providing insight into how my experience with Layout and Responsive Design. Layout and Responsive design is actually both useful and helpful in many ways. For starters, having a webpage with a layout and responsive design will make the design of the webpage more compatible with other devices while also making the design unique and dynamic, which boosts user experience. As people discover and explore the webpage, they will begin to enjoy the experience and engage, which would also gain attraction, not to mention it allows people who have disabilities, whether it is visual, cognitive, motor, or auditory, to experience the webpage like everybody else without having to face any disadvantages. This makes it fair, as it allows you to be able to enjoy the experience of websites without any stress. My experience implementing layout and responsive design has also been hectic, but I am slowly getting the hang of it and how to implement features more effectively. It does require a lot of practice, though not bad to learn and is worth the experience.
