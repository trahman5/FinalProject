How to reduce ethical issues in web development

In this list, I will provide some strategies to be able to reduce ethical issues happening throughout webpages in general:

  • Make sure the webpage is accessible to all users, ensuring people who have impairments to use the website freely
  • Ensure integrity when it comes to protecting users' data and private information
  • Implement and improve security parameters inside webpages to be able to reduce both attacks and breaches from happening
  • Follow all the necessary guidelines and regulations while also following the law to be able to not get into any sort legal trouble
  • Provide content that follows ethical standards and guidelines for the users. Doing this will result in promotion of both fairness and respect
